        A chatbot is a computer program that is designed to simulate conversation with human users, especially over the internet. Chatbots can be programmed to understand natural language input from users and provide responses or actions based on that input. Chatbots can be found in many different forms, including as part of messaging apps, on websites, or as standalone applications. They are often used for customer service, sales, and marketing purposes, as well as for personal assistance, entertainment, and educational purposes. There are different types of chatbots, including rule-based chatbots, which operate based on pre-defined rules and responses, and AI-based chatbots, which use artificial intelligence and machine learning algorithms to learn from user interactions and improve their responses over time.
